Programs

Something for every archer

Two registered USA Archery programs, built for beginners and experienced archers alike.

JOAD Club · Ages 8 to 20

Youth Archery

Our Junior Olympic Archery Development (JOAD) club gives young archers a strong foundation. Two groups based on experience, so every kid trains at the right level.

Beginner group for all new youth archers
Intermediate and tournament group for advancing archers
Beginner start required unless approved by staff
Pin achievement awards for measurable progress
Adult Archery Program · All Ages

Adult Archery

Our Adult Archery Program (AAP) is open to adults of any experience level. Whether you have never held a bow or want to sharpen your competitive edge, you belong here.

Complete beginners always welcome
Recreational and competitive training available
Equipment provided for class use
Pin achievement awards and monthly pin shoots

How it works

Easy to get started

No experience needed. Here is what joining On Site Archery looks like.

01

Reach out to Bob

Send an email or give us a call. We will answer your questions and help you figure out which program fits best.

02

Start in beginner

New archers start in the beginner program. We provide all equipment for class, so just show up ready to learn.

03

Progress at your pace

As your skills grow, you will learn more advanced techniques. Earn pins, join shoots, and compete when ready.
